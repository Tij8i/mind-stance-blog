## ***Who’s Behind This Practice***

I’ve always been drawn to understanding—not just through books and degrees, but by testing ideas in real life.

I’ve earned two master’s, worked across startups and corporates, led teams, launched ventures. I speak several languages and keep learning more.

But what shaped me most?

The yoga mat. The aikido mat. The kitchen counter. A snowboard edge. A kite line in the wind. The awkwardness of a new language.

That’s where ideas meet breath. Where thinking turns into rhythm.





### **🌍 A Life Made of Practice**

I travel regularly. I move with curiosity.

I live between the pull of stability and the thrill of change.

I’m raising a family. I love food, silence, books, beauty. I study economics, psychology, philosophy. I follow the threads of meaning wherever they lead — and then I come back to the simplicity of routine.

Over time, I’ve found that what makes life rich isn’t how much we control, but how we show up. And to show up fully, we need practices. Anchors. Routines that connect us to ourselves and to something larger.

### **🧭 Work, Too, Is a Practice**

I believe business can be more than income — it can be a training ground.

Done consciously, it sharpens you. It reveals your patterns. It challenges your ego. It asks you to grow.

That’s how I use business today. Not just to provide for my family or enjoy freedom — though it does that too — but to refine my stance, to serve others, and to stay awake to what matters.
