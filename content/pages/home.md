# This is my Practice

#### *Reflections and rituals for a grounded, adventurous way of living.*

































## We are how we are

Many Eastern traditions speak of the 道 (Dao) — the Way.

Not a destination, not a rulebook, but a rhythm. A path that you follow by letting it shape you. I subscribe to that tradition — not as a monk, not as a mystic, but as someone walking a Western path to the same tune. I live in the world, with its noise and demands, and I try to meet it with awareness.

I’ve built things. Let go of things. Tried to change the world — and then tried to just be in it. What remains is my path through life and with it my reflections.

































## Life as a Practice




Practice is not reserved for monks or athletes or writers. We’re all practicing something every day — even when we engage in the most mundane activities.

Finding room to grow in our mind is the challenge, finding a path to growth is where it all starts.

In these pages I offer my reflections on topics and activities that are meaningful to me and that help me walk my path every day. As an inspiration and a reminder of how important it is to keep searching and looking at the world with fresh eyes.

Child-like, as we naturally do when we embark on a new adventure.
